Equilibrium Support

Equilibrium is a self-contained offline puzzle for iPhone. No account is required.

How to play

Tap two neighbouring reservoirs. Their combined liquid divides as evenly as possible. If one unit remains, it goes to the clockwise reservoir. Balance all six in the fewest moves.

Progress and records

Campaign progress and best results are stored only on the current iPhone. They are not synced between devices. Deleting the app also deletes its local progress.

Motion and accessibility

Tilt or gently shake the iPhone to move the liquid. With Reduce Motion enabled in iOS Settings, the liquid remains calm and decorative transitions are reduced. VoiceOver announces every reservoir’s quantity and the purpose of each control.
